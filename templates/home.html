FAQs

Got questions? I've got answers.

What is your typical counseling process? +

Our process usually begins with deep listening and psychological assessment to fully understand your emotional needs and core struggles. This is followed by a tailored cognitive intervention and emotional healing plan, providing continuous support and feedback.

How long does a typical support cycle take? +

Depending on individual circumstances and specific needs, a typical psychological support and self-reconstruction cycle takes anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks. We flexibly adjust the pace based on your actual recovery progress.

Do you provide clinical medication? +

We focus primarily on psychological counseling, emotional support, and cognitive-behavioral interventions. For complex mental health conditions requiring clinical intervention or medication, we will recommend and assist you in connecting with professional psychiatric institutions.

Can you help with severe workplace or academic anxiety? +

Absolutely. We have helped many individuals in high-pressure environments process their emotions and rebuild their inner peace, effectively reducing the anxiety and mental burnout caused by workplace challenges and academic stress.

How do I get started? +

Simply reach out via email or leave a message on our website! We can schedule a relaxed 30-minute intro chat to listen to your thoughts, discuss your needs, and see if we are the right safe haven for you.
